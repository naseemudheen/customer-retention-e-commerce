Introduction

Welcome to the comprehensive documentation for Custkeep's E-commerce Churn Detection API. This guide is designed to assist developers and business analysts in understanding how to seamlessly integrate and optimally use our advanced API. By leveraging sophisticated machine learning algorithms, our API provides powerful insights into customer behavior, predicting potential churn to help you strategize more effectively. Whether you are aiming to enhance customer engagement or reduce churn rates, our documentation provides all the necessary details to integrate our API with your existing systems.

In this documentation, you will find detailed sections on authentication, request and response formats, and descriptions of both single and bulk prediction endpoints. We include example requests to demonstrate how to structure your calls to the API and expected responses to help you understand the data you will receive. For ease of use, we also detail the specific data fields required for submitting customer information and explain how to interpret the predictions returned by our API. Our goal is to ensure that you can quickly integrate our churn prediction capabilities into your e-commerce platform, enabling you to take proactive steps towards retaining your valued customers.

Authentication

All API requests require the use of a token for authentication. The token must be included in the header of each request.

Authorization: Token "YOUR_API_KEY"

API Endpoints

1. Single Prediction

Endpoint: POST http://localhost:8000/api/predict/

Header:

Content-Type: application/json
Authorization: Token "YOUR_API_KEY"

Request Body:

Parameter Data Type Choices/Validation Description
customer_id Char - Unique identifier for the customer.
tenure Integer - Number of years the customer has been active.
preferred_login_device Char Mobile, Computer Customer's preferred device for logging into the platform.
city_tier Integer 1,2,3 City classification tier based on size and economic indicators.
warehouse_to_home Float - Distance from warehouse to customer's home in kilometers.
preferred_payment_mode Char Debit Card, COD, Credit Card, E wallet, UPI Preferred method of payment by the customer.
gender Char Female, Male Gender of the customer.
hour_spent_on_app Float - Average number of hours the customer spends on the app daily.
number_of_device_registered Integer - Total number of devices registered to the customer's account.
prefered_order_cat Char Laptop & Accessory, Fashion, Mobile, Others, Grocery Main category of items the customer prefers to order.
satisfaction_score Integer - Customer's overall satisfaction score with the service.
marital_status Char Single, Divorced, Married Marital status of the customer.
number_of_address Integer - Number of addresses registered under the customer's account.
complain Integer 0 (No), 1 (Yes) Indicates if the customer has made any complaints.
order_amount_hike_from_last_year Float - Percentage increase in the customer's order amount compared to the previous year.
coupon_used Float - Number of coupons used by the customer during transactions.
order_count Integer - Total number of orders placed by the customer.
day_since_last_order Float - Number of days since the customer last placed an order.
cashback_amount Integer - Total cashback amount received by the customer on purchases.
